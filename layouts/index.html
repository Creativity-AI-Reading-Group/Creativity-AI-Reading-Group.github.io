AI & Creativity Research Group

Exploring Creative Reasoning, Expression, and Evaluation in LLMs

Welcome to the LLM Creativity Study Group, a collaborative space for exploring how large language models (LLMs) think, create, and inspire.

We meet to read, discuss, and experiment with ideas that connect human creativity with computational intelligence.

Mission

To understand, model, and evaluate creativity in LLMs by bridging NLP, cognitive science, and art.

Core Themes

  • Creative language generation (stories, poetry, analogy, metaphor)
  • Creative reasoning and problem-solving
  • Evaluation science (measuring originality, fluency, flexibility)
